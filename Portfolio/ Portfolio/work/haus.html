HAUS Hotel: Comfort Meets Character

Website / Brand Identity — Academic Project

Role UX & Brand Designer
Type Academic
Deliverables Brand Identity, Website Design

Overview

HAUS is a concept bed and breakfast hotel brand developed as an academic case study. The project explored how a small hospitality business could establish a premium yet approachable identity through thoughtful brand design and a considered digital experience.

The Brief

Design a complete brand identity and website experience for a boutique hotel that communicates warmth, character, and modern hospitality. The design needed to appeal to design-conscious travellers seeking something more personal than a chain hotel.

My Approach

Starting from brand strategy, I developed the visual language — including typography, colour palette, and brand voice — before translating these into a cohesive website design. The process involved competitor analysis, persona development, and multiple rounds of iteration.

Key Outcomes

  • Distinctive brand identity with a warm, editorial aesthetic
  • Responsive website design optimised for the booking journey
  • Clear typographic hierarchy and accessible colour system
  • Photography art direction guidelines for future use
